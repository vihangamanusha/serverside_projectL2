About Dr. Catherin Hamlin

Dr. Catherine Hamlin was a renowned obstetrician and gynecologist, known worldwide for her pioneering work in treating
obstetric fistula. With over five decades of dedicated service, she co-founded the Addis Ababa Fistula Hospital, transforming
the lives of countless women through her expertise in fistula surgery. Dr. Hamlin's contributions to maternal health
have had a lasting impact, especially in resource-limited regions.

Specialties and Procedures:

  • Obstetric Fistula Repair
  • Maternal and Reproductive Health
  • Gynecological Surgery
  • Preventive Obstetrics
  • Comprehensive Maternal Health Programs
