About Dr. Devi Shetty

Dr. Devi Shetty is a highly renowned consultant in cardiology with over 20 years of experience in treating patients with heart
conditions. His expertise spans a wide range of cardiovascular diseases and treatments, from preventative care to advancedheart
surgeries. Known for his compassionate approach, Dr. Shetty places great emphasis on patient education and individualized
care, ensuring that each patient fully understands their treatment options.
