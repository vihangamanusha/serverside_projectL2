About Dr. Ben Carson

Dr. Ben Carson is an esteemed dermatologist with extensive experience in diagnosing and treating a wide variety of skin
conditions. Known for his compassionate care and dedication to patient well-being, Dr. Carson specializes in both medical
and cosmetic dermatology. With over fifteen years of practice, he has helped numerous patients achieve healthier skin and
improve their quality of life through personalized treatment plans.

Specialties and Procedures:

  • Acne Treatment
  • Skin Cancer Screening and Management
  • Psoriasis and Eczema Management
  • Cosmetic Dermatology (Botox, Fillers)
  • Laser Skin Treatments
  • Anti-Aging Procedures
